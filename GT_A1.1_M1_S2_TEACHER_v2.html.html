📐Grammar — How many? / What's your…? — click to fill⏱ 8–10 min
📌 Teacher Note
Keep SHORT. Push the student to use numbers from their actual job. 'How many people on your team?' is worth more than any textbook exercise.
📐 Questions with Numbers
How many + noun + do/does + subject + have?  ·  What's your + noun?

⚡Wrap-up & Error Correction⏱ 3–5 min
Common errors — A1.1 · Numbers & Spelling
❌ How much people?
→
✅ How many people?
People = countable → How many. 'How much' is for uncountable things.
❌ My number phone is 461…
→
✅ My phone number is 461…
In English: adjective + noun. Phone number, not number phone.
❌ I have tree meetings.
→
✅ I have three meetings.
'Tree' is a plant. The number is 'three' /θriː/.
