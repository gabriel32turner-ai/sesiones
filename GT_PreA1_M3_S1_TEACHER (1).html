📋Session Guide
📌 M3 introduces action verbs. This is a big moment — the student goes from nouns (colors, numbers, names) to verbs (eat, work, sleep). Keep the gestural approach — mime everything.
🔁
0–5 min — M0 Review: Quick 5-question hot seat from M0 content. Colors + numbers + "My name is / I'm from".
⚡
5–17 min — Actions: Present 10 verbs with gestures. Hear → gesture → repeat. Then teacher gestures silently → student says the word.
🕐
17–27 min — My Day: Timeline walk-through. "Do you do this?" → Yes/No answers. Then "What do you do at 7am?" → single word answers.
🎮
27–37 min — Match It: Student plays independently. Say the word before tapping. Reset and play twice.
🧩
37–45 min — Build It: Sentence builder. Teacher reads the words in random order — student arranges and says the sentence.
🗣
45–50 min — Say It: Rapid-fire prompts. Goal: 5 automatic responses.
🏆
50–55 min — I Know This: Full progress bar including M0. Count together.
🎯
Session 1 Success Goal
Student says at least 6 action words without hesitation and attempts "I [action]" for at least 3 of them.
