🔵Warm-up — M2 Speed Review⏱ 5–7 min
THIS SESSION · Office Describe and Guess

Teacher describes an object using only M2 vocabulary. Student guesses the object. Then switch. Example: It is on your desk. It is in front of your screen. It is black. There is one of them. What is it? — The keyboard!

Name 3 things on your desk: these are my ___ .
Where is the printer in your building?
Give the plural of: company / person / country.
Is there a kitchen? What's in it?
Describe something far: that ___ is ___ .
Name 2 possessives and use them.

Key Verbs — M2
WordMeaning (español)Example

M2 grammar summary
Possessives: my/your/his/her/our/their
Prepositions: on/in/under/next to/in front of...
There is/are: 1 thing / 2+ things
This/That/These/Those: close/far, 1/2+
Plurals: +s, +es, y→ies, irregulars

Top M2 errors — reinforce in M3
❌ In front the door
→
✅ In front of the door
Always: in front OF. Most compound prepositions need a preposition after.
❌ There is two chairs
→
✅ There are two chairs
Count first. 2+ = there ARE.
