About This Program
This training program follows the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR), the international standard for language proficiency used by corporations, universities, and governments worldwide. Each level (A1.1 → B2) represents a measurable, internationally recognized milestone in language competency.

Sessions are delivered using a Communicative Approach — learners speak from day one using real professional scenarios, meeting language, and business vocabulary relevant to their roles. All materials are proprietary and developed specifically for this organization's context.
