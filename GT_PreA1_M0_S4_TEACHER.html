📋Final Session Guide
📌 S4 is the graduation of Module 0. Treat it as a celebration from start to finish. The student started 4 sessions ago saying nothing. They now introduce themselves in English. That is transformative for an adult learner with a history of failure.
🔁
0–10 min — Full review: Fast-fire all cards. No hesitation mode. Celebrate every correct response.
🎯
10–20 min — Hot Seat: 12 rapid prompts. Teacher reads, student answers. Keep the energy high and fast.
🌟
20–33 min — Capstone: Student says full introduction WITHOUT the screen. Tap each phrase they say correctly. Bonus: full conversation role-play.
🏅
33–40 min — Badge: Show the achievement screen. Read the list together. Take a screenshot together for their records.
🏆
40–45 min — I Know This: Final word count. The bar should be 80–100% full. Make this the most celebrated moment of the module.
💡 What to say at the end of S4:
"You started Module 0 with zero English words. Today you counted to ten, named colors, introduced yourself, and had a real conversation. That is not nothing. That is everything. Module 1 starts next class."
