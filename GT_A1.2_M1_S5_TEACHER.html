Key Verbs — M1 (inf → past)
WordMeaning (español)Example
work → worked 🔊trabajar ()I worked on the report all day.
finish → finished 🔊terminar ()We finished the project on time.
go → went 🔊ir ()I went to the client meeting.
have → had 🔊tener ()We had an important meeting.
make → made 🔊hacer ()She made a great proposal.
get → got 🔊obtener ()We got the contract last week.
call → called 🔊llamar ()I called the client twice.
attend → attended 🔊asistir ()Did you attend the conference?

M1 grammar summary
Regular: worked, finished, called
Irregular: went, had, made, got, came
Negative: didn't + base verb
Question: Did + subject + base verb?
Time: yesterday / last / ago / first / then

Hi Carlos, how did your week go? — Really intense! I had four client meetings, two in person and two online. I went to Queretaro on Tuesday for a factory visit. The client was very happy with what they saw.

Did you close the deal? — Not yet. I sent them a formal proposal yesterday. I worked on it for two days. Last time we tried with this client — six months ago — they said no. But this time the offer is much better.

What didn't go well this week? — We had a problem with a delivery. The order didn't arrive on time and I had to call the supplier three times. I finally sorted it out, but it took all Thursday morning. — Sounds like a tough week! But it sounds like you handled it well. — Thanks. I'm just happy the week is over!
