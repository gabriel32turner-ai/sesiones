🎯Practice — Say It8 min
📌 Teacher Note: Teacher reads each prompt. Student responds immediately in English with one phrase. No writing. No reading. Just speaking. This is the key moment — connecting image + prompt + speech in real time.
PROMPT 1
Teacher shows 🇲🇽
Student says: "Mexico" or "I'm from Mexico"
PROMPT 2
Teacher asks: "What's your name?"
Student says: "My name is [...]."
PROMPT 3
Teacher shows 🇺🇸 and asks: "Are you from here?"
Student says: "No! I'm from Mexico!"
PROMPT 4 — CHALLENGE
Teacher asks: "What's your name AND where are you from?"
Student says TWO phrases: "My name is Ana. I'm from Celaya."

📋Session Guide
📌 S3 introduces the first real English phrases the student will say in their life. Keep the energy celebratory. Every correct phrase is a milestone.
🔁
0–3 min — Review S1+S2: 5 images → colors. 5 fingers → numbers. Yes/No question.
👤
3–13 min — My Name Is: Present 5 phrases one by one. Hear → repeat → say with own name.
🌍
13–23 min — Countries: Show flags → say names. "Are you from [...]?" → Yes/No answers.
🗣
23–35 min — Role-play: Two full conversations. Slow → normal → without screen. 3 rounds.
🎯
35–40 min — Practice: 4 prompts. Student produces phrases in real time.
🏆
40–45 min — I Know This: Full S1+S2+S3 vocabulary. Count together. Big celebration.
🎯
Session 3 Success Goal
Student says "My name is [...] and I'm from [...]" without looking at the screen. That is a complete, real English sentence. That's everything.
