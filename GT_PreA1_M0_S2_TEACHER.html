📋Session Guide
📌 Session 2 starts with 2-min S1 review. Colors only — no sentences. Then numbers. Then Yes/No. Keep energy high throughout.
🔁
0–2 min — S1 Review: 5 color images. Student says the word. Fast, fun.
🔢
2–14 min — Numbers: Present 1–10 with fingers + click to hear. Student repeats each one. Then: teacher holds up fingers → student says number. Then reverse.
✅
14–22 min — Yes/No: Hear both words, then Yes/No drill with visuals. Keep questions simple and a little funny to reduce anxiety.
🎮
22–30 min — Count It: Student plays the counting game. Say the number aloud BEFORE clicking the answer.
🗣
30–40 min — Say It: Teacher shows image/fingers. Student says one word. Zero sentences needed.
🏆
40–45 min — I Know This: Mark all S1 + S2 words. Count together. Celebrate loudly.
🎯
Session 2 Success Goal
Student counts 1 to 5 without hesitation and says Yes and No instantly. Plus remembers at least 6 colors from S1.
🎲 Extra — Number Bingo (1 player)
Teacher says a number in Spanish. Student says it in English immediately. Then reverse — teacher says in English, student holds up that many fingers. Speed increases each round.
