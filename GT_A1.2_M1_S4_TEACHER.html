📐Grammar — Time Expressions in Past — click to fill⏱ 8–10 min
📌 Teacher Note
No new verb form — just positioning. Two common errors: 'yesterday I working' (wrong tense) and 'I did it ago 2 days' (wrong word order).
📐 Word Order with Time Expressions
Position: beginning OR end — both are correct
✅ Yesterday, I attended a conference.  /  I attended a conference yesterday.
✅ Last week we finished the project.  /  We finished the project last week.
❌ I did the report ago 2 days. → I did the report 2 days AGO. (ago always at the end)
❌
Yesterday I was work on the report.  ·  I did it ago 3 days.  ·  Two days ago I am in Monterrey.

⚡Wrap-up & Error Correction⏱ 3–5 min
Common errors — A1.2 · Time Expressions
❌ I did it ago 3 days.
→
✅ I did it 3 days ago.
AGO always goes at the end, after the number. Never before.
❌ Yesterday I was working on the report.
→
✅ Yesterday I worked on the report.
Yesterday + simple past (-ed/irregular), not past continuous. Keep it simple at A1.2.
❌ First I finish, then I go.
→
✅ First I finished, then I went.
Sequence words don't change the tense. Past events = past tense verbs.
