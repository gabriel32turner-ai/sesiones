🔵Warm-up⏱ 5–7 min
THIS SESSION · "Name Tag"

Teacher shows a blank name tag on screen (drawn in chat or Zoom whiteboard). Student fills it in English: name, job, company, city. Then they read it out loud. Teacher models first: "My name is Gabriel. I work at GT English Training. I'm from Celaya."

Why: immediately connects English to real identity. The student says their own information — most motivating possible content.

I work at ___.

⚡Wrap-up & Error Correction⏱ 3–5 min
Common errors — A1.1 · Greetings & Introductions
❌ My name Carlos.
→
✅ My name is Carlos.
The verb 'to be' is never dropped. Every sentence needs a verb.
❌ I am work in sales.
→
✅ I work in sales.
Don't use 'am' + verb. Simple present = 'I work', not 'I am work'.
❌ I am from the Mexico.
→
✅ I am from Mexico.
Country names don't use 'the' — except: the USA, the UK.
