📐Grammar — Verb TO BE8–10 min
The verb TO BE — am / is / are
I am · He / She / It is · You / We / They are
Examples
I am Ana. I am from Mexico.
She is my manager. He is Carlos.
We are a team of five. They are from Germany.
You are in the sales department.
⚠ Common mistakes
❌She am from Mexico. → She is from Mexico.
❌I is Ana. → I am Ana.
❌They is my colleagues. → They are my colleagues.
