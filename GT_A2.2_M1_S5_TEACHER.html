Key Words — M1
WordMeaning (español)Example
culture 🔊workplace values and behavior (cultura laboral)Our company has a collaborative culture.
environment 🔊the atmosphere where you work (ambiente)I prefer a relaxed work environment.
hybrid 🔊mix of remote and office work (hibrido)We've already moved to a hybrid model.
benefits 🔊extras beyond salary (beneficios)Our benefits include health insurance.
achievement 🔊something you successfully completed (logro)My biggest achievement has been building the team.
experience 🔊things you have done and learned (experiencia)I've had a lot of international experience.
personality 🔊the type of person you are (personalidad)I tend to be direct but empathetic.
passion 🔊something you love deeply (pasion)I'm passionate about continuous learning.
Key Structures — M1
WordMeaning (español)Example
have/has + PP 🔊present perfect — relevant now (presente perfecto)We've already moved to hybrid work.
already/yet/just 🔊PP adverbs — time/relevance (ya / todavia / justo)I've just finished / Have you met her yet?
tend to 🔊to usually do something (soler / tender a)I tend to work best under pressure.
be into 🔊to enjoy something (estar interesado en)I'm really into photography.
for / since 🔊PP duration / starting point (por / desde)I've worked here for 3 years / since 2021.
ever / never 🔊PP life experience (alguna vez / nunca)Have you ever worked remotely?
come across as 🔊to seem to others (dar la impresion de)She comes across as very calm.
be known for 🔊to be recognized for a quality (ser conocido por)He's known for being reliable.
Photo
M1 grammar summary
PP + already/just: middle position
PP + yet: end of sentence
PP: still relevant / Past: specific time
Tend to: subject + tend(s) to + verb
For/since/ever/never with PP

A lot has changed at my company in the last two yearsen los ultimos dos anos, and I've noticed a huge difference in how people feel about coming to work. We've already moved to a hybrid model — three days in the office, two from home. The company has just launched a wellness program and I've signed up for the gym allowance. I've also been given more responsibilities since last year — I haven't asked for a formal promotion yet, but I think it's coming.

I tend to perform best in environments that are results-orientedorientados a resultados but not micromanagedmicromanejados. I consider myself ambitiousambicioso and reliableconfiable — people tend to trust me with important things.

Outside of work, I've recently started meditation. I've been doing it for about two months and it's helped with stress. I've never had a job that felt this alignedalineado with who I am. And that makes all the difference.

Top M1 errors — reinforce in M2
❌ We already moved / I work here for 3 years
→
✅ We've already moved / I've worked here for 3 years
Present perfect needs HAVE/HAS. Both are ongoing — not finished.
❌ I tend be direct / Since 3 years
→
✅ I tend TO be direct / For 3 years
Tend to needs TO. Since needs a point in time. For needs duration.
