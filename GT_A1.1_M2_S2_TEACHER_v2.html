Prepositions of Place
WordMeaning (español)Example
on 🔊touching the top surface (sobre / encima)My laptop is on the desk.
in 🔊inside a space (dentro de / en)The papers are in the folder.
under 🔊below something (debajo de)My bag is under the desk.
next to 🔊beside something (al lado de)My phone is next to my keyboard.
in front of 🔊facing something (enfrente de)The screen is in front of me.
behind 🔊at the back (detras de)The printer is behind the door.
between 🔊in the middle of two (entre)My monitor is between two plants.
near 🔊not far from (cerca de)The meeting room is near reception.
Key Verbs
WordMeaning (español)Example
put 🔊to place something (poner)Put the folder on the shelf.
move 🔊to change position (mover)Move your chair next to the window.
find 🔊to discover where (encontrar)I can't find my phone!
leave 🔊to put and go away (dejar)I left my notebook in the meeting room.
look for 🔊to search for (buscar)I'm looking for the USB cable.
keep 🔊to store in a place (guardar)I keep my files in the drawer.
pick up 🔊to lift from a surface (recoger)Can you pick up that pen?
point at 🔊to indicate (senalar)She pointed at the map.

📐Grammar — There is / There are — click to fill⏱ 8–10 min
📌 Teacher Note
1 thing = there IS, 2+ things = there ARE. Count first then choose.
📐 There is / There are
THERE IS → singular (1)
There is a printer on this floor.
Is there a kitchen? Yes, there is.
THERE ARE → plural (2+)
There are two screens on my desk.
Are there chairs? Yes, there are.
❌
There is two printers. · There are a meeting room.

2+ things → there ARE
Question: Is there / Are there?

⚡Wrap-up and Error Correction⏱ 3–5 min
Common errors — A1.1 · Prepositions and There is/are
❌ The printer is in front the door.
→
✅ The printer is in front of the door.
Always: in front OF. Same: on top of, in the middle of.
❌ There is two printers.
→
✅ There are two printers.
Two printers = plural → there ARE.
❌ My desk is near to the window.
→
✅ My desk is near the window.
Near does not need to. Just: near + noun.
