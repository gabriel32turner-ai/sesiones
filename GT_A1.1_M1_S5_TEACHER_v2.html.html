Key Words — M1
WordMeaning (español)Example
name / surname 🔊first and family name (nombre / apellido)My surname is Garcia.
job / company 🔊what you do / where you work (trabajo / empresa)My job is in sales.
team / manager 🔊your colleagues / your boss (equipo / jefe)My team is 8 people.
country / nationality 🔊where from / nationality (pais / nacionalidad)I am Mexican.
number 🔊a figure (numero)My phone number is 461 147 2119.
small talk 🔊friendly light conversation (charla informal)We do small talk before meetings.
meeting 🔊a work gathering (reunion)I have three meetings this week.
weekend 🔊Saturday and Sunday (fin de semana)Any plans for the weekend?
Key Verbs — M1
WordMeaning (español)Example
be (am/is/are) 🔊identity / description (ser / estar)I am Ana. She is my manager.
work 🔊to have a job (trabajar)I work at a manufacturing company.
live 🔊to have a home (vivir)I live in Celaya.
speak 🔊to use a language (hablar)I speak Spanish and a little English.
spell 🔊to say letters (deletrear)Can you spell your name?
come from 🔊to be originally from (ser de)I come from Guanajuato.
ask 🔊to make a question (preguntar)What do you ask a new colleague?
introduce 🔊to present someone (presentar)Let me introduce myself.

→
✅ I work / She IS German.
Verb to be: never drop it. Simple present: no am before action verb.

→
✅ Where ARE you from?
Wh- questions always need the verb.
