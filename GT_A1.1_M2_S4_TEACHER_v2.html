+ s
most words

chair → chairs
+ es
after s,x,ch,sh
bus → buses
box → boxes
y → ies
consonant + y
company → companies
country → countries
Irregular: person → people · man → men · woman → women · child → children

⚡Wrap-up and Error Correction⏱ 3–5 min
Common errors — A1.1 · M2 Integration
❌ The company have many equipments.
→
✅ The company has a lot of equipment.
Equipment = uncountable — no plural. And: it → has not have.
❌ I borrow to you my charger.
→
✅ Can I borrow your charger?
Borrow = take. Lend = give. These are opposites and are often confused.
❌ There is no chairs in the meeting room.
→
✅ There are no chairs.
Chairs = plural → there ARE no chairs.
