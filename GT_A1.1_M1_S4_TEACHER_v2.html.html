📐Grammar — Wh- Questions — click to fill⏱ 8–10 min
📌 Teacher Note
Wh- questions are the engine of all conversation. At A1.1: What / Where / Who / When with to be and do/does. Focus on the verb after the Wh- word — students always forget it.
📐 Wh- Questions — What / Where / Who / When
WHAT
things / names / jobs
What is your name? What do you do?
WHERE
places / locations
Where are you from? Where do you work?
WHO
people
Who is your manager? Who is that?
WHEN
time / days
When is the meeting? When do you start?

⚡Wrap-up & Error Correction⏱ 3–5 min
Common errors — A1.1 · Small Talk & Wh- Questions
❌ Where you from?
→
✅ Where are you from?
Wh- questions ALWAYS need a verb. Where + ARE + you.
❌ What is you do?
→
✅ What do you do?
For action verbs use DO/DOES. What do you do? = what is your job?
❌ I am agree, it is busy week.
→
✅ I agree. It is a busy week.
Agree needs no am. And busy week needs the article a.
